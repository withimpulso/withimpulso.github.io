1. Introduction

This website is operated by Impulso. We use cookies and similar technologies only when necessary to ensure the website functions correctly and to gain insight into website usage in a privacy-friendly way.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help websites function correctly and, in some cases, provide insights into how a website is used.

3. Cookies and analytics we use

3.1 Functional cookies (always active)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly (for example, basic navigation and security). They do not store personally identifiable information and do not require consent under applicable law.

3.2 Analytics with Plausible

Impulso uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics solution hosted in the EU. Plausible is cookieless and is designed to avoid collecting personal data.

  • No cookies used for analytics
  • No tracking across websites
  • No personal profiles created
  • Aggregated, privacy-friendly insights

Because Plausible does not use cookies or track personal data, consent is typically not required under GDPR. You can read more in Plausible’s privacy information.

3.3 No marketing or tracking cookies

Impulso does not use advertising cookies, marketing cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

4. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling functional cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or legislation. The latest version will always be available on this website.
