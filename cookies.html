This website is operated by Impulso. We use cookies and similar technologies only when necessary to ensure the website functions correctly and to gain insight into website usage in a privacy‑friendly way.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help websites function correctly and, in some cases, provide insights into how a website is used.

2. Functional cookies

We use functional cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function properly (for example, basic navigation and security). These cookies do not store personally identifiable information and do not require consent under applicable law.

3. Analytics with Plausible

Impulso uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy‑friendly analytics solution hosted in the EU. Plausible is cookieless and designed to avoid collecting personal data.

  • No cookies used for analytics
  • No tracking across websites
  • No personal profiles created
  • Aggregated, privacy‑friendly insights

Because Plausible does not use cookies or track personal data, consent is typically not required under GDPR.

4. No marketing or tracking cookies

Impulso does not use advertising cookies, marketing cookies, or third‑party tracking cookies.

5. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling functional cookies may affect website functionality.
